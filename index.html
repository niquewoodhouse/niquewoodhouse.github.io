WIREWAX

WIREWAX is a free to use video editing product that works in any modern web browser.

1. Drag any video file onto the site, or paste any video URL from the big video sites like YouTube.

You can use it to make anything in any video interactive by just drawing around it. When you've drawn around something, you decide what appears/happens when a viewer interacts.

Since 2013 I've had the huge pleasure of designing the platform & future features.
